About my thesis

During my internship with TotalEnergies in Esbjerg, Denmark I have worked in Logistics and operations department where I was in a direct contact with the vessels supplying the offshore oil and gas installations. I was given an opportunity to go offshore and investigate why are there differences between the routes the planning department scheduled for the ships and the routes that the ships have actually taken. I have enjoyed this experience and was awarded the prize for the best bachelor thesis for this project.
